My research centers around nonlinear wave phenomena. I am especially interested in mathematical aspects of
nonlinear acoustics and the use of mathematical tools in the development of different ultrasound applications.
Particular topics include:
  • Well-posedness and regularity of nonlinear acoustic models
  • Error analysis of numerical discretizations
  • Absorbing boundary conditions for open-domain problems
  • Optimization of ultrasonic focusing devices
  • Efficient simulation of ultrasonic waves
