My research centers around nonlinear wave phenomena. I am especially interested in mathematical aspects of nonlinear acoustics and the use of mathematical tools in the development of different ultrasonic applications. Particular topics include:
  • Well-posedness and regularity of nonlinear acoustic wave models
  • Error analysis of finite-element discretizations
  • Absorbing boundary conditions for open-domain problems
  • Optimization of ultrasonic focusing devices
